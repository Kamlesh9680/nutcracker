Privacy Policy

At Nutcracker, we value the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and services, which allow users to upload videos, generate unique shareable links, and earn rewards based on video views.

By using our website and services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Effective Date: 10/12/2024

  • 1. Information We Collect:

    We collect the following types of personal information when you use Nutcracker:
    • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, and payment information to facilitate rewards processing.
    • Video Content: The videos you upload to Nutcracker are stored on our servers. You retain ownership of your video content, but by uploading videos, you grant Nutcracker a license to use, display, and distribute your videos.
    • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited.
    • Reward Information: We collect data related to your earnings, including video views and any related statistics necessary for calculating your rewards.

  • 2. How We Use Your Information

    Your information is used for the following purposes:
    • To Provide Services: We use your account and video information to provide our services, including uploading, sharing, and tracking video views.
    • To Process Rewards: We use your usage data and reward-related information to calculate and distribute earnings.
    • To Improve Our Services: We analyze your usage data to improve the functionality and user experience of Nutcracker.
    • To Communicate with You: We may send you emails about your account, rewards, and important updates.

  • 3. How We Protect Your Information

    We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information and video content from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

  • 4. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:
    • With Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist with video hosting, analytics, and reward processing.
    • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from public authorities.

  • 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze usage patterns, and personalize content and ads. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

  • 6. Your Rights and Choices

    You have the right to:
    • Access and Correct Your Information: You can view and update your personal information by logging into your account.
    • Delete Your Account: If you no longer wish to use our services, you can request account deletion. Please note that some data may remain in our archives for legal or operational reasons.
    • Opt-Out of Communications: You can opt out of marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send.

  • 7. Children's Privacy

    Nutcracker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.
